CMS Admin Theme Project

This project is a mock Admin website I was designed to flex my skills in Bootstrap, HTML5, and CSS3. It contains the following webpages: A home page, Login page which you go to when you click on the logout link at the top right corner. The login page is validated but not authenticated. So you'll have to fillout the form. Then Pages, Posts and Users which you go to by clicking on the links on the navbar respectively. There is also an edit page for pages and posts if you click the 'edit' button on the pages respectively. You can also fillout a mock form when you click on the 'create account' dropdown on the users, posts and pages webpages respectively.

You are welcome to check it out @:WWW.mycmsadminthemeproject.com

See source code in github@:mygithub
